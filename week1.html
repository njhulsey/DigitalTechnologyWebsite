Week 1: Web Design

Challenge 1

Our first challenge was to build a tower to hold a marshmellow and whoever had the tallest tower won. We started off with the idea of of making a pyramid, but decided on making a prism because it would allow for a stronger base. Towards the top of the tower we had the four corners meet in the center, creating a prism-like structure. We doubled the spaghetti noodles making them able to hold the marshmellow's weight. We actually ended up winning by having the tallest tower.

Challenge 2

Our second challenge was to build a ramp to move a marble: whoever made the marble travel the longest won. At first we wanted to build a long ramp at the end of the table, but ended up changing our design well over the half way point. The new design was a really steep ramp that just free shot the marble into a basket, however the tower was too tall and wasn't stable enough to hold the marble. I think changing our design was why we failed, it was thought out enough.
