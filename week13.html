Week 13: Final Project

Final Projects

For my final project I wanted to make a planter that will water itself when the dirt is dry. I started this project weeks before but had not really made any progress on it until the final week. My circuit board did not end up working because of having a motor in series with a 5v regulator. I had to make my own with a perfboard. The planter works and I currently have strawberries in them. Some of the issues with the planter was the glue and water proofing and also the circuit board. I learned a lot about transistors and that I still have a lot to learn before mastering electronics.
